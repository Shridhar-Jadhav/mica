MICA’s Online Programmes

“Refresh, Upskill, and Thrive with MICA’s Online Programmes”

  1. Home
  2. Programs
  3. Online Programmes
  1. Overview

MICA believes in the democratisation of knowledge. Over the past many years MICA has been offering courses with our ed tech partners to ensure that the knowledge we create reaches everyone who wants it. If you wish to refresh your skills, MICA has a course for you. If your objective is to learn a new skill, we have a variety of certifications that enable you to do just that. To succeed in the world today, whether it be industry, policy, academics or the social sector, having a growth mindset is a must. Continuous learning is the hallmark of a successful career, and we at MICA want to ensure that everyone has access to it, not constrained by space or time, so that you can learn wherever and wherever you want.
