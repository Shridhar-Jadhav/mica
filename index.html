The Foundation

At MICA, we are dedicated to being a true ‘school of different thought,’ where we cultivate not only creative and analytical thinkers but also visionary leaders. Our mission is to sharpen our students' management skills, preparing them to excel in corporate and organizational leadership while sparking their entrepreneurial spirit. Here, We do not simply make good leaders. We also make good citizens.

Read more

Mission

MICA shall be the Premier Management School for Marketing, Communication, and Creativity in a design-driven and digitally enabled world.

Vision

To develop empathetic leaders and thinkers who are creative and adaptive problem-solvers for a rapidly changing world.

Culture & Values

MICA’s core values center on empathy, transparency, inclusiveness, ethical integrity, societal and environmental responsibility, participative management, a global mindset, and a commitment to excellence.
